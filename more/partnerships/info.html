Requirements + Rules

  1. You must be a registered Org or Non-Profit.
  2. You must be able to help or support in some way (Grants, Donations, Food Trucks, etc.)
  3. Your Org or Non-Profit should not support any homophobic, racist, or otherwise diregatory businesses.
  4. Members of your Org or Non-Profit should follow your code of conduct, and during YAC Events, our code of conduct.

Notice

  • We reserve the right to terminate partnerships with anyone at will for any reason.
  • Youth Action Council™ is not responsible for injuries of Org/Non-Profit members during events.
  • YAC™ is considered an Official School Club, therefore legal issues should be brought to GHHS attention.
