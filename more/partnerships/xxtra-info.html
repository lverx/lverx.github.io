Extra Information

The Youth Action Council™ is an Official High School Club based at Grand Haven High School in Michigan. We only accept partners with Michigan-Local Orgs and Non-Profits. We may waive these restrictions if you contact us (YAC makes no guarrantee). For higher chance of restrictions being waived, look at our Waiving Reasons below.

Waiving Reasons

  • Entities tied to or within an Government Agency.
  • Entities that are well known or well respected and are a large corporation or business.
  • Entities that can supply mission-critical items otherwise inaccesible to YAC (Email Us).
  • Entities with legal networks (Legal Councils, Lawyers) may be subject to enwaivement.
